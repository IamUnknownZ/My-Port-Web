About Me

Hello there! At Triamudomsuksapattanakarn Nonthaburi School, I'm Kanokpong Hukham, a student. Like other children, my interest in computers began when I was young. Apart from my technological pursuits, I have a strong interest in the moral implications of using technology in the classroom. I'm committed to promoting a deliberate and moral approach to integrating computers into educational settings because I believe that creativity and responsible use must coexist in harmony.
